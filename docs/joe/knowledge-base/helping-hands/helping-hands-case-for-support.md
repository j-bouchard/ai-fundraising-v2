# HELPING HANDS FOOD BANK
## Case for Support

---

### THE PROBLEM

**1 in 7 people in Greater Metro face hunger every day.**

That's 150,000 of our neighbors - including 45,000 children and 18,000 seniors - who don't know where their next meal will come from.

Despite living in a region of abundance:
- A minimum wage worker needs 82 hours of work to afford monthly groceries
- 35% of seniors choose between food and medicine
- 1 in 4 children only eat regular meals when school is in session
- Food costs have risen 40% while wages increased only 15%

**Hunger is hidden in our community.** It's the teacher buying snacks for students. The elderly neighbor whose Social Security doesn't stretch. The family choosing between rent and groceries.

---

### OUR SOLUTION

For 30 years, Helping Hands Food Bank has been the region's first line of defense against hunger.

**We provide more than meals - we provide hope and dignity.**

#### Emergency Food Distribution
- **5,000 families** receive food monthly
- **2.5 million pounds** distributed annually
- **$10 of groceries** for every $1 donated
- **3 distribution sites** + mobile pantries reaching food deserts

#### Kids Backpack Program
- **2,000 students** receive weekend food packs
- **50 schools** partnered across the region
- **89% of teachers** report improved Monday focus
- **Zero children** should learn hungry

#### Senior Nutrition
- **500 seniors** receive home delivery
- **Monthly nutrition workshops** for healthy aging
- **Medical partnerships** for food prescriptions
- **94% remain** living independently

#### Community Gardens
- **200 families** grow their own produce
- **50,000 pounds** of fresh vegetables annually
- **Job training** in urban agriculture
- **Building community** while fighting hunger
