# HELPING HANDS FOOD BANK
## Strategic Plan 2024-2027

---

### OUR MISSION
To eliminate hunger in the Greater Metro area through food distribution, nutrition education, and advocacy.

### OUR VISION
A community where no one goes to bed hungry and everyone has access to nutritious food.

### OUR VALUES
- **Dignity**: We serve with respect and compassion
- **Equity**: We ensure fair access to resources
- **Community**: We build partnerships to multiply impact
- **Stewardship**: We maximize every dollar and donation

---

## STRATEGIC PRIORITIES 2024-2027

### PRIORITY 1: EXPAND ACCESS
**Eliminate food deserts and reach underserved populations**

**Goals:**
- Open 2 new distribution sites in South and East Metro by 2025
- Increase mobile food pantry routes from 3 to 6 by 2026
- Serve 7,500 families monthly by 2027 (up from 5,000)

**Key Metrics:**
- Families served monthly
- Geographic coverage (zip codes served)
- Pounds of food distributed

**Investment Needed:** $750,000 over 3 years

---

### PRIORITY 2: DEEPEN IMPACT  
**Move beyond emergency food to address root causes**

**Goals:**
- Launch job training program in food service/culinary arts
- Expand nutrition education to 1,000 participants annually
- Connect 500 families to SNAP benefits and resources
- Grow community gardens from 5 to 8 sites

**Key Metrics:**
- Program participants completing training
- Families enrolled in SNAP
- Garden plot utilization rate
- Participant reported health improvements

**Investment Needed:** $500,000 over 3 years

---

### PRIORITY 3: STRENGTHEN SUSTAINABILITY
**Build financial resilience and operational excellence**

**Goals:**
- Grow revenue from $3.5M to $5M by 2027
- Build operating reserve to 6 months expenses
- Increase monthly donors from 150 to 500
- Achieve 85% donor retention rate

**Key Metrics:**
- Total revenue growth
- Reserve fund balance
- Monthly donor count
- Donor retention rate
- Cost per meal provided

**Investment Needed:** $250,000 (fundraising infrastructure)
